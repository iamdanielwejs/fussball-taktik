# Product Vision

## Vision Statement

**For** children aged 7 to 12,
**who** play football or are interested in it,
**Fussball-Taktik is** a browser-based, interactive simulation
**that** explains fundamental tactical concepts like man-marking, zone defense, and shifting toward the ball
in a playful and visual way.
**Unlike** static tactic boards or complex professional tools,
**our product offers** a child-friendly, animated experience with drag-and-drop interaction
that invites experimentation and learning.

## Target Audience

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Age | 7-12 years |
| Prior Knowledge | Basic understanding of football, no tactical experience required |
| Technical | Tablet or desktop browser, touch and mouse input |
| Context | Self-directed learning, club training, school lessons |

## Core Goals

1. **Understanding over memorization** - Children should grasp tactical concepts through hands-on experimentation
2. **Visual and interactive** - Animated players that respond to user input
3. **Low barrier to entry** - Instantly usable without registration or installation
4. **Fun while learning** - Child-friendly design with colors, sounds, and positive reinforcement

## Success Criteria

- A child can explain the difference between man-marking and zone defense after 5 minutes of use
- The application runs smoothly on common tablets (iPad, Android tablets)
- No adult assistance needed to operate the app
- Load time under 3 seconds

## Scope

### In Scope
- 2D top-down view of a football pitch
- Players as figures with drag-and-drop
- Tactical concepts: man-marking, zone defense, shifting toward the ball
- Predefined formations (4-4-2, 4-3-3, 3-5-2)
- Learning mode with step-by-step explanations
- Tactic comparison view
- Export as image
- Sound effects

### Out of Scope
- 3D rendering
- Multiplayer / real-time collaboration
- Full match simulation
- User accounts / backend
- Professional tactical analysis
